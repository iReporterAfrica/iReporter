Report a Red Flag Or Intervention

Are you being oppressed, bullied or subjected to hazardous/unfair conditions? perhaps you aren't but know someone or people who are. Whatever it is you think requires immediate Intervention in your organization or your Environment, just report it

 

We will Investigate

We are very aware of fake news peddlers and people who could use the platform to met undue justice or raise false alarm hence we would carry out thorough investigation before intermediating as applicable. Note that the consequence for false alarm or fake news can range from rejection of report to complete ban from the platform or involvement of the law. This depends on the severity of a false report.
That said, genuine and authentic reports are always welcomed

 

Act Accordingly

The team would would reach out to the decision makers involved with the reported case after investigation and possibly publish report. Our reporters with their permission stand a chance of being recognized or awarded for their diligence and country man spirit.
